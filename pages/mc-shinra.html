SHINRA KUSAKABE

Shinra Kusakabe exists within a world where all hell has literally broken loose and fire breathing demons run the lands. Some people, pyrokinetics, are born with the power to either create fire or control. Keeping all of this madness in check are the ever ready Fire Force Companies. Shinra was born as a Third and Fourth Generation pyrokinetic. He would end up in the service of Special Fire Force Company 8. After his mother's death, and supposedly his younger brother's as well, Shinra joined the Special Fire Force to achieve his goal of becoming a hero, saving people from Spontaneous Human Combustion, and uncovering the truth behind his family's death twelve years ago. It is later revealed that Shinra possesses the Adolla Burst, making him The Fourth Pillar.

Shinra Kusakabe is one of my favorite heroes because of his personality. As an anime fan, you get used to the timid and shy hero archetype that must find their true selves on the final season after 100 episodes of being a cry baby. I'm most talking about Deku from My Hero Academia. I swear I hate that kid. Thankfully, Shinra is the complete opposite. From the moment he arrives on scene, this kid is ready to fight. I love how tenacious he is portrayed and how he is always ready to fight. Sometimes this could be to a detriment. This reminds me of possibly my favorite scene from the entire show. Upon first meeting Benimaru, believing him to be nefarious, Shinra exclaims, "I'm gonna kick your ass". This seems tough, but unknown to Shinra at the time, Benimaru would have probably killed him very easily in a real fight. But you just gotta love that tenacity. Shinra's power is just as great. To be able to literally kick the holy hell out of someone with feet of fire is absolutely insane. This kid is literally leaving the Devil's Footprints on your face.
