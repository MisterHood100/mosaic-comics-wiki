SUPERMAN

Born on the dying planet of Krypton, baby Kal-El was sent to Earth in a spaceship by his parents, Jor-El and Lara, who foresaw the coming destruction of their planet. After Landing on a farm in Smallville, Kansas, he would be taken in and raised by the farm owners, Jon and Martha Kent. They would try their best to raise him as a normal boy name Clark but with the super powers that he gained from the Earth's sun, it would be nearly impossible. As he would grow older, he would realise that he couldn't just stand by and allow injustices to happen if he had the power to stop it. This would lead him to becoming a defender of justice known as Superman.

In my eyes, what always made Superman an incredible superhero and one of my personal favorites is the mere fact that he is the absolute embodiment of what we think of when we here the word, superhero. He's the smiling, morally guided and justice serving golden boy with every power and ability under the sun... don't even ask me how. And you're talking about the best origin story possible.
