HULK

Dr. Bruce Banner, Ph.D. is an American theoretical physicist, famed for his work into the studies of nuclear physics and gamma radiation. He was recruited by General Thaddeus "Thunderbolt" Ross and the US Army to develop the first Gamma Bomb. During its first live test he was bombarded with a massive dose of gamma rays while saving Rick Jones, a kid who was out on the test site. He was mutated into a green behemoth, the living personification of rage, fueled by pure physical strength and would come to be known as nearly mindless "Incredible Hulk". Fearful of the damage that Hulk could inflict, as well as fleeing from the military, he went on the run. He later found a home among the Avengers, the Earth's Mightiest Heroes, for a short time.

Hulk is one of my favorite superheroes because he is the personification of an emotion that every person has experienced at some poit, unbridled anger. I mean, even the nicest person has at some point been angered to the point where they wanted to turn green and destroy everything in sight. At that point, instead of doing it in real life and getting arrested, they probably picked up a paperback of World War Hulk and watched him do it for you. Not only is Hulk a gigantic beast, but when Banner is not angry or destroying planets he is helping other superheroes, like the Avengers, with his ridiculous science capabilities. Dr. Bruce Banner is a genius in nuclear physics, possessing a mind so brilliant that it cannot be measured on any known intelligence test. The hulk doesn’t need any gimmicky weapons to be powerful. He doesn’t need a hammer, like Thor. He doesn’t need a suit, like Iron Man. He doesn’t need a bow and arrow, like Hawkeye. All he needs is to be angry. If only it was that easy in real life... Just kidding but not really.
